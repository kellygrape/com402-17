### Knowledge challenge!

```
var recipe = {
  title: "Dad's Pancakes",
  servings: 6,
  ingredients: [
      "1 1/4 cup flour",
      "1 cup milk",
      "1 egg",
      "2 tsp baking powder",
      "3 tsp butter"
  ]
};
``` 

Given the recipe above ...

- How many properties does it have?  
- What are they?
- What are their values?
- What are their data types?
- How would you access each value?
